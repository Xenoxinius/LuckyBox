The Original Fan-Powered Subscription

Founded in 2012, Lucky Box™, Inc. is the worldwide leader in fan subscription boxes. Lucky Box partners with industry leaders in entertainment, hobby's, and snacks to deliver monthly themed boxes, produce interactive experiences and digital content, and film original video productions. In its first five years, Lucky Box has delivered over 14 million boxes to fans in 35 territories across the globe.

Powered By Becode.org — We approach everything that we do with playfulness and humor, and try to create multiple layers of discovery for fans. Whether it's shipping a crate that turns into a proton-pack, or re-creating a "floating" hoverboard at 1:5 scale, everything we do is intended to bring your fandom into the real world, and to your door, in unexpected ways.
